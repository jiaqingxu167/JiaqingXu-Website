Jiaing Xu

Professional Tutor

My passion for education can be traced back to my high school days. I have been on the path of teaching from the beginning, and the reason is simple. I love learning and sharing knowledge, and it makes the world a better place.

Since middle school, I have never been the brightest kid in class. However, I still did pretty good in school and exams because I was always the most excited one about learning new things every day. Due to my wide range of interests, I would have to spend hours a day studying all the different subjects and even extracurricular things. After years of practicing, I became an expert in summarizing and isolating the core idea from the books.

I knew teaching was my true calling when I helped my friend, who used to cram hundreds of pages of notes yet struggled with exams, really understood the important concepts and aced the next exam. That is when someone first told me that I have a knack for breaking complicated problems into small pieces and easy concepts, and helping people forget about all the complex formulas and techniques for every different type of problems.

Education furnishes us with knowledge about the world, helps us develop our values and perspective of life, and equips us with the tools to make our lives better. The idea of infinite possibility in the future still excites me even up to now, and I would love to share that possibility and excitement with others for the rest of my life.
